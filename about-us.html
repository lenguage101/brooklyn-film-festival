Theme of the Festival, July 25th

The theme has finally been determined and we are happy to announce that this year’s theme is the Marvel Universal. The 3 main characters to be connected with the Marvel Universe are Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor. With each of these character, having their own trilogy, it will truly be an action-packed 4 days so do not miss your chance!

Pre-Registration, July 24th

Hey there! Pre-register in our contact page to gain access to the film festival. The first hundred to register will be given to the privilege of priority seating. Pre-registering not only ensures your guaranteed entrance to the film festival but it keeps you up-to-date with future announcements first and provides you more information about the festival, fellow sponsors, and other entertainments.

Introducing the Festival, July 23rd

We are excited to announce the creation of the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival. The Brooklyn Outdoor Film is scheduled to take place from August 5th to August 8th. The festival is being sponsored by the Brooklyn Vibes Events. Co whose founder is Jennifer Viala and as well as the organizer of said festival. The location is the Brooklyn Bridge Park. The theme is the festival is to be determine. However what has been established so for is that the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival’s attendance is completely free and has a maximum seating of up to 5,000. Bookmark the page is future announcements. Thank you and look forward to seeing you there!
